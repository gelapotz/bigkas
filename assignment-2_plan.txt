## Platform

The app is made for **Android** and designed to feel natural on mobile devices. The layout takes inspiration from language‑learning apps like Merriam‑Webster and Drops, focusing on clarity, readability, and simple interactions.

---

## User Interaction

Bigkas is built around **tap‑based navigation**. Users interact with cards, buttons, and icons to explore vocabulary in quick, bite‑sized ways. The experience is intentionally simple and familiar, especially for anyone who has used modern learning apps.

Recent improvements focused on making the app feel more unified. All screens now use the **same blue app bar**, with consistent spacing, icon sizes, and titles. This creates a smoother flow when moving between Home, Decks, Discovery, Word Details, Quiz, Challenges, and Settings.

The app encourages learning through:

- Tapping cards to reveal meanings or examples  
- Browsing themed vocabulary decks  
- Exploring curated words in the Discovery section  
- Completing quizzes and weekly challenges  
- Navigating back using the unified app bar  

Overall, Bigkas offers a clean, approachable way for people to learn Tagalog in fun, manageable pieces.
